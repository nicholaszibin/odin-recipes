Lasagne

Description

A delicious lasagne made by Wikipedia

Ingredients:

  • 1 box pasta noodles
  • 4 large cans (500ml) of tomatoes
  • 1 kg lean ground Beef
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 large carrots
  • 1 cup of cheese

Directions:

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Make Sauce
  3. Combine in layers
  4. Add cheese to the top
  5. Bake at 350 degF for 5 years
